Project Overview

What is BodyFocus?

BodyFocus creates personalized, adaptive workout plans that respect your body's constraints and evolve with your progress. Unlike generic workout apps, it:

  • Prioritizes safety: Filters exercises based on knee (or other joint) limitations
  • Adapts daily: Pre-workout check-ins adjust volume based on energy and pain levels
  • Focuses on effectiveness: 4-Hour Body philosophy (minimal effective dose)
  • Learns over time: (v0.3+) AI interprets feedback to refine future plans
Tech Stack
  • Swift 5.9+
  • SwiftUI (iOS 17+)
  • JSON Storage (v0.1)
  • Swift Package Manager

v0.1 Core Features

Smart Onboarding

Capture goals, injury history, schedule, and equipment in 4 simple steps. Generate your first personalized workout plan in under 3 minutes.

Knee-Safe Workouts

Automatic exercise filtering by knee load level. Sensitive knees? No problem. Only get exercises that respect your constraints.

Pre-Workout Check-Ins

Feel tired? Knees hurting? The app automatically adjusts your workout volume based on how you feel today.

Set-by-Set Tracking

Mark each set complete, flag painful exercises, and track your progress. Your data stays safe on your device.
